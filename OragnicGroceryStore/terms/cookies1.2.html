Cookies

We use cookies on our website to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They allow us to remember your preferences and to personalize your experience on our website. We reserve the right to modify this cookie policy at any time. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They allow us to remember your preferences and to personalize your experience on our website. Cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

How we use Cookies?

We use cookies on our website to improve your experience. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences and to personalize your experience on our website. We also use cookies to track how you use our website and to improve our website and services.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through the settings of your web browser. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
